Flash15

Flash15 is a new app that offers real time flash deals to customers in their specific zip code.

Flash deal drops based upon your interest level and zip code. The only caveat is you have 15 minutes to purchase the flash deal or it expires.

Flash15 is quite simple:

Customer: Choose up to 4 zip codes for deal drops and check out categories of interest which include:

Restaurants Massage/Nails/Hair/Waxing Retailers Clothing/Shoes/Accessories Entertainment

Sit back, don’t do anything and wait for deals to drop on your phone. You will then have 15 minutes from the time the deal dropped to click on the deal and purchase the offering or do nothing.

Business:

Choose up to 4 zip codes that you want to reach, choose category, and enter a deal for customers in that specific zip code. Deals are based on a 15 minute timer and below are just some examples:

Buy one large pizza and get a small cheese pizza for free
25% Vans t-shirts
20% manicure and pedicure
Buy one 6 inch sandwich and get a side of french fries free
